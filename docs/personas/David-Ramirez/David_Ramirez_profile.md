## Biography

David Ramirez is an IT Operations Manager with two decades of experience leading infrastructure, service desk, and platform operations teams. He built his career by moving from hands-on systems administration into operational leadership, where he became known for stabilizing high-pressure environments and building reliable support models.

Across healthcare, manufacturing, and public-sector organizations, David has led incident response programs, service maturity initiatives, and cross-functional operating models that improved uptime and reduced escalation fatigue. He regularly partners with security, network, and application teams to keep service delivery aligned with business priorities.

David's leadership style centers on consistency, accountability, and mentorship. He believes strong operations are built through clear expectations, useful metrics, and teams that are empowered to solve problems before they become outages.
