## Biography

Tyler Bennett is an IT Help Desk Technician II focused on frontline support, endpoint troubleshooting, and user enablement. He started in customer service and quickly transitioned into technical support, where his ability to stay calm and communicate clearly became a core strength.

He supports a broad range of day-to-day issues including account access, device performance, Microsoft 365 troubleshooting, and onboarding workflows. Tyler consistently improves resolution outcomes by documenting fixes, refining ticket quality, and collaborating with systems and security teams on recurring problems.

Tyler believes great support combines technical accuracy with empathy. He prioritizes restoring productivity quickly while ensuring users understand what happened and how to avoid repeat issues.
