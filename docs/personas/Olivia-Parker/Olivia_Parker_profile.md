## Biography

Olivia Parker is an IT Project Manager with deep experience delivering infrastructure, security, and modernization initiatives in complex enterprise environments. She began her career as a business analyst, which shaped her ability to translate technical constraints into clear business language.

Over the years, Olivia has led cross-functional programs involving cloud migration, endpoint refreshes, identity hardening, and data center transitions. She is known for keeping high-visibility projects on track by building practical plans, clarifying dependencies, and resolving conflicts early.

Olivia combines structured project discipline with strong interpersonal leadership. She believes project success depends on transparent communication, realistic timelines, and steady alignment between technical teams and business stakeholders.
