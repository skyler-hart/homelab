## Biography

Sarah Chen is a Junior Systems Administrator supporting Windows infrastructure, endpoint management, and Microsoft 365 operations. She began her career in desktop support and quickly moved into systems work by taking ownership of recurring endpoint and identity issues.

In her current role, Sarah helps maintain Active Directory hygiene, device compliance, patching routines, and day-to-day server administration tasks. She collaborates closely with senior administrators to improve runbooks and reduce manual support effort through scripting and standardization.

Sarah is thoughtful, dependable, and growth-minded. She values clean documentation and steady skill development, and she is working toward becoming a well-rounded systems engineer with strong automation habits.
