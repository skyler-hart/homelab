## Biography

Emily Carter is an IT Help Desk Technician II specializing in end-user support, onboarding, and Microsoft 365 administration. She entered IT early through campus support roles and developed a reputation for combining technical troubleshooting with exceptional user communication.

In enterprise support environments, Emily has worked across account lifecycle tasks, Teams and Outlook incidents, endpoint compliance checks, and remote assistance workflows. She is frequently trusted with user-facing initiatives because she can turn complex technical instructions into clear, practical guidance.

Emily's approach is empathetic and process-aware. She aims to resolve issues quickly while improving documentation and reducing repeat confusion for both users and support teams.
