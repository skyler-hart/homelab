## Biography

Jacob Foster is a Junior Network Engineer building hands-on experience in enterprise routing, switching, and wireless operations. He entered IT through help desk and NOC support roles, where he developed a strong troubleshooting foundation and interest in network design.

He now supports day-to-day network operations, change execution, and incident response under senior engineering guidance. Jacob has contributed to switch refreshes, wireless tuning efforts, and standardized monitoring improvements in multi-site environments.

Jacob is eager, disciplined, and growth-oriented. He learns quickly from real incidents and values strong documentation, lab practice, and mentorship as the path to long-term engineering excellence.
