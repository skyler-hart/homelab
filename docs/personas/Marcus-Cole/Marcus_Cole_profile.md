## Biography

Marcus Cole is a SOC Analyst III with deep experience in threat detection, incident response, and threat hunting. He began in security monitoring and gradually moved into advanced investigation roles where he became known for disciplined analysis and high-confidence triage.

He has supported enterprise SOC operations across regulated environments, improving detection coverage, refining escalation playbooks, and reducing analyst fatigue through better tuning and workflow design. Marcus regularly partners with infrastructure and engineering teams to close visibility gaps and improve mean time to contain.

Marcus is patient, precise, and data-driven. He believes strong security operations depend on clean telemetry, documented response workflows, and analysts who can separate noise from meaningful attacker behavior.
