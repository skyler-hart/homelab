## Biography

Ryan Mitchell is a Senior Network Engineer focused on enterprise routing, switching, wireless, and perimeter security. He started in data center operations and grew into network architecture and escalation engineering roles where precision and speed were equally important.

He has designed and supported resilient network environments for distributed organizations, including hybrid WAN, segmentation initiatives, and firewall modernization programs. Ryan is often the go-to engineer for complex outages involving asymmetric routing, intermittent latency, and cross-domain troubleshooting.

Ryan is pragmatic and evidence-driven. He values clean design, clear documentation, and repeatable change practices that reduce downtime while keeping growth and performance in view.
