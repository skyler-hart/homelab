## Biography

Ethan Blake is a Senior Cloud Architect specializing in enterprise cloud transformation, landing zone design, and infrastructure automation. He transitioned from systems engineering into architecture after leading early hybrid-cloud adoption programs and seeing firsthand how governance and design decisions shape long-term reliability.

He has guided organizations through multi-cloud platform strategy, identity integration, workload migration, and operating model modernization. Ethan is known for creating pragmatic architecture standards that balance security, developer velocity, and operational sustainability.

Ethan approaches architecture as a long-term capability, not a one-time project. He emphasizes reusable patterns, guardrails, and measurable outcomes so engineering teams can move faster without sacrificing control.
