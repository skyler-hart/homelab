## Biography

Jordan Cross is a Principal Infrastructure Engineer specializing in enterprise infrastructure, automation, identity platforms, and operational resilience.

Over the course of his career, Jordan has supported federal agencies, defense organizations, and large enterprises operating mission-critical environments. His experience spans Active Directory, Microsoft 365, Entra ID, virtualization, automation, hybrid cloud, identity integration, enterprise messaging, cybersecurity operations, and platform engineering.

Jordan built his reputation by modernizing legacy infrastructure without disrupting operations. He is known for translating complex technical challenges into practical, maintainable solutions that reduce operational burden while improving reliability.

While many engineers focus on a single technology stack, Jordan's strength comes from understanding how systems, networks, security controls, automation platforms, and business requirements intersect.
